4. Manufacturing Process Documentation

The SGAI-APEX-3P is manufactured entirely at our Bhiwadi, Rajasthan facility (total area: 42,000 sq.ft. built-up). The manufacturing flow is as follows:

StageProcessEquipment / Process ControlIn-Process QC Checkpoint
1 Raw Material Receipt & Inspection NABL-certified incoming inspection lab; SiC ceramic blanks from Hindustan Ceramics (Pune); UHMWPE panels from Garware Hi-Tech Films, Nashik Dimensional check, material cert. verification, density test
2 Ceramic Tile Preparation CNC waterjet cutting (Omax 60120); surface grinding; edge radiusing CMM dimensional verification; edge inspection under 10x loupe
3 UHMWPE Laminate Pressing Hot-press (HPRC-600T); cycle: 120°C, 500 psi, 45 min; layup in clean room (Class 10,000) Thickness gauge; void detection via ultrasonic C-scan
4 Ceramic-Composite Bonding Two-component polyurethane adhesive (Huntsman Araldite AW 109); vacuum bag curing; 24 hr room-temp cure Bond strength test (ASTM D1002); adhesion peel test (sample per lot)
5 Carrier Assembly Industrial sewing machines (Brother S-7250A); Cordura cutting (Eastman Eagle A-1); MOLLE webbing laser-welded at stress points Seam strength test per MIL-C-83489; MOLLE pull test (≥ 225 kg)
6 Soft Armour Panel Integration UHMWPE panel cut and inserted; DWR-treated cover stitched Panel alignment; cover seam inspection; waterproof spray test
7 Final Assembly & QR System Fit Plate insertion; QR buckle attachment; size labelling; QR code application Release time measured; weight; visual inspection per 100% acceptance plan
8 Batch Ballistic Witness Testing 1 unit per 100 units submitted to TBRL / authorized range for witness fire. Sample per lot. Witness firing report enclosed with delivery
9 Packing & Dispatch Vacuum-sealed plates; individual CFB box; palletisation Packing list cross-check; barcode scan; final weight verification
